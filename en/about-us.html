About Us

ARTIŞ Endüstriyel Temizlik Ürünleri

As ARTIŞ Endüstriyel we have been operating in industrial cleaning, chemicals, paper products and similar fields since our establishment in May 2024. By closely following the innovations and technological developments in the sector, we have made it our mission to offer the highest quality and environmentally friendly products to our customers. The main purpose of our organization is to provide sustainable and effective solutions to industrial hygiene and cleaning needs. In line with our aim, we aim to keep customer satisfaction at the highest level, constantly expand our product range, and strengthen our leading position in the sector by giving importance to the training and development of our employees. In addition, we act in accordance with the principles of environmental sustainability to increase our environmental awareness and leave a more livable world to future generations. In this context, we aim to keep our carbon footprint to a minimum by using recyclable and nature-friendly products. With social responsibility awareness, we also attach importance to contributing to the welfare of the society by supporting various social responsibility projects at local and national levels. All of these efforts aim to set an example for other companies in our industry and to establish long-term, trust-based business relationships with our customers.
